# Entering Scores

Open **Enter Match Scores** from a match card when you are allowed to enter or correct scores.

![Enter Match Scores](/help/captain/screenshots/enter-match-scores.png "Enter Match Scores")

## Actual players

Select the actual players who played each line. Players must have a valid rating for the division rating type before they can be selected as actual players.

## Saved setup teams

If saved Match Setup teams are enabled for the division, you can apply a saved team to a score line. Saved teams that contain a player without a valid rating are hidden until that player receives a valid rating.

## Submit for verification

When all required games have scores, submit the match. The opposing captain or co-captain reviews the scores and either validates or disputes them.

![Completed matches](/help/captain/screenshots/completed-matches.png "Completed matches")

## Match score details

Use **Match Score Details** to review the final result, line winners, player names, and game scores after a match is verified.

![Match score details](/help/captain/screenshots/match-score-details.png "Match score details")

## Correcting submitted scores

Scores that are pending verification can still be corrected by the submitting captain until the opposing captain validates or disputes them.
