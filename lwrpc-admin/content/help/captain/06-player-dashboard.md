# Player Dashboard

The Player Dashboard gives you quick access to your teams, standings, match schedules, play history, and DUPR information.

## Signing in

Sign in with the same email address you use for Club Membership. If this is your first time using the system, select **Forgot Password** to receive a password setup email. You can also use a passkey or fingerprint sign-in when it is available on your device.

## My Teams

Each team card shows team contacts, the team record, roster, and league documents. Select **Captains** to email a team contact.

- Select **Team Roster** to see current team members, ratings, individual records, and contact information.
- Select **My Play History** to see your completed games and statistics. Use the Play History Scope pull-down to filter by team, league, or season.
- Select **Division Standings** to review rankings, records, and point differentials. You can choose another team from the header pull-down, then select a team name to view its full division schedule and completed matches.
- Select **League Documents** to expand the available documents for that team.

## Team Matches

Select **Team Matches** in My Teams to see upcoming and completed matches, including opponents, locations, and schedules. Use **Show Complete Matches** to display completed matches and their Match Results.

Select **Match Details** to review an upcoming match, open a map for the match location, or view either team roster. Completed matches include game-by-game results and winners.

## DUPR Ratings

Season DUPR ratings appear throughout the system. To learn how DUPR ratings are calculated, visit [DUPR: How It Works](https://www.dupr.com/how-it-works). For questions about the Club's rating policy, contact info@lwrpickleballclub.com.

## Mobile use

The system is optimized for mobile devices and tablets. On a phone, the Player Dashboard also provides **Install the LWR PB Club System** instructions so you can add a shortcut icon for easier access.
