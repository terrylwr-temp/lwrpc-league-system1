# Team Rosters

The Team Roster screen shows the players currently assigned to a team, their rating status, and recent play history.

![Team Roster Management](/help/captain/screenshots/roster-management.png "Team Roster Management")

## Adding players

Use **Add Player** to select from eligible members. The player list is filtered by the selected home location and excludes anyone already on the roster.

![Player Pulldown](/help/captain/screenshots/Player-Pulldown.png "Player Pulldown")

- **Eligible** means the player has the required rating and fits the team rating range.
- **Rating Needed** means the player can be added to the roster, but cannot be used for Match Setup or score entry until the required rating is entered.
- **Not Eligible** means the player cannot be added because they do not meet the roster eligibility rule, such as no DUPR ID or being outside the division rating range.

## Home community filtering

Some leagues restrict roster adds to the team home community. When that rule is enabled, captains, co-captains, and club pros see the **Player Home Location** filter locked to the team home location. League Managers and Commissioners can override the filter.

## Rating check notices

If you add a player who has a missing season rating, the system sends a rating check email to league support. You will receive an update when the rating is entered.
